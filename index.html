How to Play

  • Your goal is to place exactly one queen in each row, column, and color region.
  • Tap once to place an X (no queen here), tap twice to place a queen, tap again to clear.
  • Queens cannot touch each other, not even diagonally.
  • Every puzzle you see is guaranteed to be solvable.
